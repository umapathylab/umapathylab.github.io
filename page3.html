Our Approach

  • Conservation Genetics
  • Biodiversity Genomics
  • Conservation Physiology
  • Conservation Breeding

We use polymorphic DNA markers and phylogenetics to establish the population structures, relatedness, genetic diversity & gene flow in the wild populations.

We use Next-generation sequencing and Bioinformatics to address conservation related problems and answer intriguing questions in ecology and evolution.

We are specialized in the detection of steroid hormones in non-invasive samples for assessing the reproductive status and stress levels in multiple species.

We use artificial insemination and captive breeding to revive populations of endangered species which are at the brink of extinction.
